The Seven Wonders Of The World

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.



Great Wall Of China

The Great Wall of China stretches over 13,000 miles and was built to protect against invasions. It is one of the most iconic structures in the world.



The Petra Of Jordon

Petra is a historical and archaeological city in southern Jordan, famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system.



Christ The Redeemer

The Christ the Redeemer statue in Rio de Janeiro is a symbol of Christianity and overlooks the city from the 700-meter Corcovado Mountain.



Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is an ancient Incan city located in the Andes Mountains in Peru. It is known for its sophisticated dry-stone construction and panoramic views.



Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza is a complex of Mayan ruins on Mexico's Yucatán Peninsula. It was a major regional center and is famous for its pyramid, El Castillo.



Colosseum

The Roman Colosseum is a massive amphitheater in Rome, Italy. It was used for gladiator contests and public spectacles in ancient Rome.
